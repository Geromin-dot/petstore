# Research Notes

## Domain Analysis
- **Pet Store Requirements**: Need for categorization, search functionality, and a seamless checkout process.
- **Inventory Management**: Pets need status tracking (Available vs Sold) to prevent double-selling.

## Technical Choices
- **Spring Boot**: Chosen for its robust ecosystem and ease of creating REST APIs.
- **React**: Chosen for its component-based architecture and fast UI updates.
- **H2 Database**: Used for development due to its zero-config requirement, with plans for PostgreSQL in production.
